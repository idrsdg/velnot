### Ürün Adı:
Velnot

### Tagline (80 karakter):
Record any meeting. Get an AI action plan. No bot. No integrations.

### Kısa Açıklama (150 karakter):
Windows desktop app that records meetings, transcribes them, and generates structured AI action plans. No bot joins your call.

### Uzun Açıklama:
Velnot is a Windows desktop app that records any meeting — Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, phone calls, any audio — transcribes it accurately, and generates a structured AI action plan with decisions, next steps, and owners.

No bot joins your call. No calendar integrations. No browser extensions. Your recordings stay on your machine. One click from the system tray.

Key features:
- One-click system audio + mic recording
- Accurate transcription (AssemblyAI Universal-2)
- AI action plan: decisions, action items, owners
- 14 languages (UI + transcription)
- Speaker identification
- Export to text, markdown, PDF, Word
- Backup & restore
- Works with any meeting software

Pricing: Free tier (3 sessions) · $10.99/mo · $89.99/yr · $219 lifetime
